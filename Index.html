How its made

These dice are created with epoxy resin and are divided into two opaque or transparent colors. Metallic gold foil leaf has been delicately inlayed into the top layer of each die.

Hand Crafted

We have found that hand crafting our dice produces the results we strive for. Pouring, sanding, inking, and polishing dice by hand allows the us to be in full control of every step of the process.

Hard edge dice can only be produced by hand as edges are lost when using machinery for mass production.

No die is perfectly balanced...
but need not worry...

Our dice are made of resin with foil inlay. The foil in each die averages around .00125 grams in weight. So while we can't completely say the dice will be perfectly balanced, t he foil weight is negligible and would have no considerable impact.
